Benjamin was a very strict professor who used to teach about investements and share market. Warren was the only student in his class who got A+ rating, given by Benjamin Graham.He asked Benjamin that he wants to work with him but Benjamin denied because Warren was very sharp and he wanted someone who is hired by no one so that he can work harder and harder. Warren never give up and then, there came a time when Benjamin finally accepted him. Warren shifted working with him.

He was so focused that when other kids were busy watching playboy magazines, he was busy reading balance sheets of companies.

After sometime, there came a stress between Warren and Benjamin. Their theories started to mismatch. They left the job with Benjamin and returned to his hometown, Omaha. He purchased his first house and ever since he has been living there till date.
